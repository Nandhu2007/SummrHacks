    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are some of the common questions we get asked

    How much is the admission fee?
    Admission to the event is completely free of charge for all attendees. We believe in making our events accessible to everyone, so there's no cost to enter.
    Do I have to bring my own food?
    No, you don't need to bring your own food. We will be providing a variety of refreshments and meals throughout the event.
    Do you need to know how to code?
    No, you do not need to know how to code. We will be providing seminars and have volunteers who will assist you throughout the process.
    How do I form my group?
    Groups will have a maximum of 5 members and will be formed on the day of the hackathon. You are welcome to decide on your group members beforehand.
    What if I don't have a team?
    If you don't have a team, don't worry! We'll help you find teammates on the day of the hackathon.
    Where is it (address)?
    340 E Middlefield Rd, Mountain View.
